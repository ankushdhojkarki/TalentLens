Is my résumé stored?

No. Files are processed in-memory and discarded after analysis. Nothing is written to disk or kept by Gemini.

What file types are supported?

How accurate is the scoring?

The model is calibrated against hundreds of recruiter-reviewed résumés. Treat the score as directional, the rewrites as immediately actionable.

Yes. TalentLens is free during the v1 launch.
